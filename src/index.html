About us

The Maria Levitskaya Ballet and Contemporary Dance Studio is a unique creative space that brings together leading professional specialists in various fields of dance, music, and acting arts. It is an arts center capable of uniting the interests of professionals and enthusiasts, where every child and adult is given the opportunity to explore and realize their full creative and artistic potential.
