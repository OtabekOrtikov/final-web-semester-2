About Us

Home / About Us

The Maria Levitskaya Ballet and Contemporary Dance Studio is a unique creative space that brings together leading professional specialists in various areas of dance, music and acting. An art center that combines the interests of professionals and amateurs, where every child and adult is given the opportunity to learn and realize their full creative and artistic potential.
The concept is based on an academic approach to all the proposed areas. Artistic director Maria Levitskaya united teachers and specialists with higher professional education, titles and awards into a single creative team.
The complex of our directions is aimed at various age categories from the youngest children from 2.5 years old and adults without restrictions. Everyone will be able to find their own way in revealing their dreams and personality.
